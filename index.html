Million Dollar TYR Cup S1


The Million Dollar TYR Cup Season 1 Grand Finals took place on July 11, 2026 at Union Mall in Bangkok, Thailand.
The tournament served as the first official large-scale offline esports competition for Ragnarok Origin Classic (ROOC).
Players and fans from different countries gathered to witness one of the biggest events in the history of the game.

A total of twenty professional players reached the championship stage after months of qualifiers and regional competitions.
The Grand Finals featured Team ENCORE from the Philippines and Team Poek from Indonesia, the two strongest teams of the tournament.

Grand Finals Match
Team ENCORE displayed excellent teamwork, communication, and strategic gameplay throughout the series.
Their coordination allowed them to dominate every match and defeat Team Poek with a clean 4-0 victory.
With this win, ENCORE became the first-ever Million Dollar TYR Cup Champions in ROOC history.

Tournament Results
Champion: Team ENCORE (Philippines)
Runner-up: Team Poek (Indonesia)
Champion Prize: USD $500,000 and the Champion's Medal
Runner-up Prize: USD $100,000

Event Highlights
The Grand Finals featured a professional esports stage with live audiences, interactive activities, photo zones, and community events.
Before the championship match, a special exhibition game involving Thailand's KOL team entertained the audience.
The event celebrated both competitive gaming and the Ragnarok community.

The tournament was broadcast live across multiple streaming platforms and attracted more than one million viewers worldwide.
Fans who could not attend in person were still able to watch the exciting matches online.

Competition Statistics
The inaugural TYR Cup attracted 333,352 players from 19 different regions.
A total of 29,472 teams competed in more than one million matches during the tournament, making it the largest official Ragnarok tournament ever held.
